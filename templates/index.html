An API for your data

Connect and share data with your workspaces, your colleagues, and your applications.

Simplify data sharing

Digital Biome is an API-first data sharing platform built for data scientists.
We give you an easy interface to open your data up to the world.

Access

Add and retrieve data frames from anywhere. You can use our open Python and R libraries to access your data in your scripts. Or, create your own interface—our system is fully RESTful.

Share

Data sharing is as easy as copying a line of code. You can manage access keys programmatically or with our online interface and control who has access to which data.

Collaborate

Give collaborators seamless access to your up-to-date data, whether you are working with other people or building a web app with Django, Flask, or Shiny.
